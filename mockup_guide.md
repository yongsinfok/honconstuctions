# Design & Mockup Guidelines (Figma/UI)

## Visual Identity
*   **Style:** Professional, Industrial, Minimalist High-Contrast.
*   **Color Palette:**
    *   **Primary Background:** White (#FFFFFF) & Dark Grey/Black (#111111) sections.
    *   **Accent Color:** Electric Blue (#0066FF) - Used for Primary Buttons, Icons, Highlight text.
    *   **Text:** Black (#0F172A) for body, White (#FFFFFF) on dark sections.
*   **Typography:**
    *   **Headings:** `Space Grotesk` (Condensed/Bold) or `Oswald`. Strong, industrial feel.
    *   **Body:** `Inter` or `Helvetica`. Clean, legible, modern sans-serif.

## Layout Structure (One-Page)

1.  **Hero Section:**
    *   **Background:** Full-width dark overlay image (Construction site / Piping detailed shot).
    *   **Center:** Bold white Headline (Condensed).
    *   **Action:** Two buttons side-by-side. Blue filled (Primary), White outline (Secondary).

2.  **Top Navigation:**
    *   Sticky (Glassmophism effect). Logo Left. Links Right (Services, Projects, About, Contact). Language Toggle (EN/CN).

3.  **Services Grid:**
    *   3x2 or 2x3 Grid.
    *   Minimalist Line Icons (Pipe, Water Drop, Excavator, Wrench).
    *   Card style: Light subtle shadow, hover lift effect.

4.  **Featured Projects (Table/Cards):**
    *   **Desktop:** Clean data table for list view (Project Name | Client | Status | Value).
    *   **Mobile:** Stacked cards.
    *   **Highlight:** "Featured" badge on top 3 big projects.

5.  **Why Us / Stats:**
    *   4-Column Icon with Text stats (35 Years, G5 Grade, 100% Compliant).
    *   Use bold large numbers.

6.  **Contact / Footer:**
    *   Simple form on Left. Contact Info on Right.
    *   Dark background.

## UI Micro-interactions
*   **Buttons:** Hover -> Slight translateY(-2px) + Box Shadow.
*   **Links:** Underline expands from left-to-right on hover.
*   **Scroll:** Smooth scroll to anchor links.
*   **Modal:** Project details open in centered modal with backdrop blur.

## Accessibility (a11y)
*   Ensure #0066FF on White meets AA contrast (4.5:1).
*   Focus states on all inputs/buttons (Outline: 2px solid Blue).
*   Skip link hidden at top.
